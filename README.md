# 📊 Online Sales Dashboard

📌 An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, and regional insights to support **data-driven business decisions** and maximize profitability.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across **regions, customer segments, product categories, and shipping modes**.  
It enables stakeholders to track key KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and make informed strategic decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

💰 Total Sales Performance: 1.4M+ peak revenue  
📊 Profit Analysis across all regions and categories  
🧾 Customer Segments: Corporate, Consumer, Home Office, Small Business  
🌍 Regional Insights: Ontario, West, Quebec, Atlantic, etc.  
🚚 Shipping Modes: Regular, Delivery, Express  
📅 Time Analysis: Daily & Monthly trends  

---

## 🔎 Key Insights

🥇 Ontario recorded the highest sales performance (~0.35M+)  
📈 Sales peak observed in January and September  
🏢 Corporate segment contributes the largest share (~36%+)  
🚚 Regular shipping is the most used method (~50%+)  
📉 Sales fluctuate with noticeable mid-month peaks  

---

## 🎯 Business Impact

- Improved visibility of overall sales performance  
- Identified top customers and profitable segments  
- Supported data-driven decision-making  
- Enhanced understanding of revenue distribution  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters (Year, Quarter, Category, Ship Mode)  
- Dynamic KPI tracking  
- Multi-dimensional analysis (Region, Time, Segment)  
- Clean and structured visual storytelling
