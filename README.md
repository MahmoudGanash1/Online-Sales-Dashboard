# 📊 Online Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze **online sales performance**, identify profitability drivers, and provide actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into a **business intelligence solution** that helps stakeholders optimize revenue and improve profitability.

---

## 🎯 BUSINESS PROBLEM

The business lacked clear visibility into:
- Sales performance across states
- Customer contribution and behavior
- Profit and loss by product sub-categories
- Impact of payment methods on revenue

This limited effective decision-making and performance optimization.

---

## 💡 SOLUTION

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that:
- Centralizes sales data into a structured analytical model
- Enables dynamic filtering by category and payment mode
- Provides state-wise revenue analysis
- Highlights top customers and key profit drivers
- Identifies best and worst performing sub-categories

---

## 🛠 TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES

- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📊 KEY FEATURES

- 🧭 Control Panel: Category & Payment Mode filtering  
- 🌍 State-wise Analysis: Amount distribution across states  
- 🏆 Top Customers: Identification of high-value customers  
- 📉 Profit & Loss Analysis: Top 5 sub-categories performance  
- 📊 Category & Payment Share: Percentage distribution breakdown  

---

## 🔍 KEY INSIGHTS

- Clear variation in sales performance across states  
- Small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue  
- Certain sub-categories generate significant losses while others drive profit  
- Payment method distribution impacts overall revenue patterns  
- Category segmentation reveals strong concentration in specific product groups  

---

## 📈 BUSINESS IMPACT

- Improved visibility of online sales performance across all dimensions  
- Enabled identification of top customers and high-impact segments  
- Helped reduce losses by highlighting underperforming sub-categories  
- Supported data-driven decision-making for pricing and sales strategy  
- Enhanced overall profitability analysis and revenue optimization  

---

## ⚡ KEY HIGHLIGHTS

- Interactive filtering (Category, Payment Mode)  
- State-level performance analysis  
- Customer-level revenue insights  
- Profitability breakdown by sub-category  
- Clean and executive-ready dashboard design
